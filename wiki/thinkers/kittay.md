# Thinker: Eva Feder Kittay

**Tradition:** Feminist philosophy; care ethics; disability justice  
**Key Works:** *Love's Labor*

---

## Core Ideas

Eva Feder Kittay is important for this wiki as a critic of contract theories that assume independent, fully reciprocating contractors. Her work emphasizes dependency, care, disability, and the social position of caregivers.

In relation to [Contractarianism](../concepts/contractarianism.md), Kittay shows that theories built around mutual advantage can undervalue both dependents and caregivers. This makes her central to the [Inclusion and Exclusion](../debates/inclusion-exclusion.md) debate.
